Passionate About Geospatial Innovation

Welcome! I'm dedicated to demystifying the world of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and making complex spatial concepts accessible to everyone. With years of experience in mapping, remote sensing, and data visualization, I create engaging content that inspires and educates.

My mission is to empower individuals and organizations to leverage the power of location intelligence for better decision-making, problem-solving, and understanding our planet. From beginner tutorials to advanced analytical techniques, you'll find a wealth of resources here.

  • Expertise in ArcGIS, QGIS, Python (Geopandas), R (sf)
  • Specialization in environmental GIS, urban planning, and public health mapping
  • Committed to clear, practical, and impactful content creation
